Cafe Recommendations in East Bay

As a former teacher, I've spent countless hours after school in cafes, grading papers, reading books, and answering emails. Since then, I have come to appreciate cafes a lot and many have asked me for my recommendation, so here they are! (They all have wifi, relatively good coffee, outlets, and easy parking.)

Allegro

Berkeley, CA

Located in a Whole Foods market, the cafe has a wide open space, free wifi, and plenty of outlets along the walls. My favorite part about this place is you can also grab food from the hot deli section in Whole Foods.

The high tables are also great for using as standing desks.

>Yelp

Kaleidoscope

Point Richmond, CA

This place feels like home. It's a small and quaint place located in Point Richmond. There are these larger tables that seat 4 people that's nice for studying in a group. The baristas are really friendly. There is also a section of the cafe with couches and beanbags. Sometimes, before setting down at the cafe, I will go take a walk at the local park.
